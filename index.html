Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This is yummy South Korean coffee that's all over of the internet only requires 4 ingredients!!! All of which YOU have in the cupboard, you feel like you've won the jackpot, right?

The Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Instant Coffee/Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

The Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar and hot water
Add 2 table spoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 two tablespoons of hot water, and mix all these in a mixing bowl.
Step 2: Whisk, and keep whisking until the coffee is light and fluffy.
Using an electric hand-held mixer or a manual mixer, whio the coffee mixture until it is light brown, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed
Serve with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a hot or cold milk with the coffee. Serve a straw, and stir. Enjoy!!!
